VitalicX

Your comprehensive fitness and nutrition companion for iOS

Available on App Store iOS

Transform Your Health Journey

VitalicX is a powerful, all-in-one fitness and nutrition tracking application built specifically for iOS. Designed with a focus on simplicity and effectiveness, VitalicX combines comprehensive tracking capabilities with an intuitive interface to help you reach your health and fitness goals.

Built using cutting-edge Swift 6+ and SwiftUI technologies with a robust SwiftData backend, VitalicX delivers a native iOS experience that's both beautiful and blazing fast. Every feature is thoughtfully designed to make health tracking effortless and insightful.

Core Capabilities

Nutrition Tracking

Track your meals with precision using our integrated barcode scanner and comprehensive nutrition database. Access USDA FoodData Central and OpenFoodFacts for accurate nutritional information on thousands of foods.

Exercise Logging

Plan and track your workouts with customizable routines, exercise libraries, and detailed logging. Create workout plans that fit your goals and track your progress over time.

Advanced Analytics

Visualize your progress with comprehensive analytics and insights. Track trends, identify patterns, and make data-driven decisions about your health and fitness journey.

Recipe Management

Build and manage your recipe collection with detailed nutritional breakdowns. Create custom recipes, save favorites, and integrate them seamlessly into your meal planning.

Meal Planning

Plan your meals ahead of time with our intuitive meal planner. Schedule recipes, track daily nutrition targets, and stay on top of your dietary goals.

Weight Tracking

Monitor your weight progress over time with detailed charts and trends. Set goals, track milestones, and visualize your journey to your target weight.

Goal Setting

Set personalized fitness and nutrition goals tailored to your needs. Track your progress and stay motivated with visual feedback and achievements.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device with SwiftData. No cloud sync means complete privacy and offline access to all your health information.

Built with Modern iOS Technologies

VitalicX leverages the latest iOS development technologies to deliver a premium, native experience with exceptional performance and reliability.

Privacy Policy

VitalicX is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the VitalicX app.

By using VitalicX, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional, stored locally)
  • Timezone

HEALTH & BIOMETRIC DATA:

  • Weight measurements with timestamps
  • Height
  • Age/Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Activity level
  • Body Mass Index (BMI - calculated)

NUTRITION TRACKING DATA:

  • Food consumption logs (meals, quantities, timestamps)
  • Custom recipes you create
  • Recipe ratings and notes
  • Daily calorie and macronutrient tracking
  • Meal timing and patterns
  • Food search history

EXERCISE & FITNESS DATA:

  • Workout sessions (exercises, sets, reps, weight)
  • Workout routines you create
  • Fitness objectives and goals
  • Preferred workout times
  • Fitness level self-assessment
  • Workout frequency preferences
  • Exercise performance history

APP USAGE DATA:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Recently used foods and recipes
  • App interactions and navigation patterns

AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED DATA:

  • Device timezone
  • App version
  • Camera access (for barcode scanning only, when granted)

We do NOT collect your location, browsing history outside the app, contacts, or any data from other apps.

We use your data exclusively to provide and improve app functionality:

  • Nutrition Tracking: Calculate daily nutrition totals and track progress toward goals
  • Health Insights: Generate personalized nutrition recommendations based on your biometric data
  • Workout Planning: Create and track exercise routines and progress
  • Food Database Searches: Query external databases (USDA, Open Food Facts) to provide nutrition information
  • App Personalization: Remember your preferences and frequently used items
  • Feature Improvement: Understand how features are used to improve the app

We do NOT use your data for:

  • ✗ Advertising or marketing
  • ✗ Selling to third parties
  • ✗ User profiling for commercial purposes
  • ✗ Tracking across other apps or websites
  • ✗ Any purpose other than app functionality

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data based on:

  • Consent: You explicitly provide health and nutrition data for tracking purposes when you use the app's features
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to provide the app functionality you requested
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving app features and user experience based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your data or uninstalling the app.

LOCAL STORAGE:

All your personal data, health information, nutrition logs, and workout history are stored LOCALLY on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework with SQLite backend. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Is protected by your device's passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID
  • Is encrypted when your device is locked (iOS native encryption)

CLOUD SYNC:

We do NOT currently offer cloud synchronization. Your data remains exclusively on your device.

We cannot access, view, retrieve, or recover your data. If you delete the app or lose your device, your data cannot be recovered unless you've created a backup through your device's backup system.

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES WE USE:

1. USDA FoodData Central API

  • Purpose: Food nutrition database searches
  • Data Shared: Search keywords and food database IDs only
  • Data NOT Shared: Your name, email, health data, meal logs, or any personal information
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.usda.gov/privacy-policy

2. Open Food Facts API

  • Purpose: Barcode product lookups
  • Data Shared: Barcode numbers only (when you scan a product)
  • Data NOT Shared: Your name, email, health data, meal logs, or any personal information
  • License: Open Database License (ODbL)
  • Privacy Policy: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

3. Apple StoreKit

  • Purpose: In-app purchase and subscription processing
  • Data Shared: Purchase transactions (managed entirely by Apple)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Foundation Foods Database

  • Type: Local database bundled in the app (USDA data)
  • Data Shared: None - entirely local, no network requests

We do NOT use:

  • ✗ Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • ✗ Crash reporting tools (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • ✗ Advertising networks
  • ✗ Social media tracking
  • ✗ User profiling services

When you use external food database features, minimal non-personal data is transferred internationally:

  • USDA FoodData Central: Data sent to US government servers (search queries and food IDs only)
  • Open Food Facts: Data sent to servers in France (barcode numbers only)

NO personal health data, meal logs, or identifying information is ever transmitted to these services. Only search terms and product identifiers are sent to retrieve nutrition information.
